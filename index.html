I'm an interdisciplinary researcher who is not afraid to learn new methods and enjoys challenges. My interests span from hardware to software, and I have also fallen in love with music.

My approach to research combines two worlds: a deep understanding of why circuits work, and the expertise to build tools that define how they are designed. Grounded in device physics and analog circuit theory, I develop sophisticated algorithms to discover optimal solutions and maximize the performance of circuit designs.

My goal isn't just to design a single circuit, but to create the automated methodologies that empower the design of countless others, ultimately pioneering the next generation of hardware for the music and audio industry.
